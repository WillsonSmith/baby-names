Ontario's Popular Baby Names

What is this?

Inspired by this article I thought I would try to build my own graphing site for data based in Canada. To do this I parsed data Publicly Available from the government of ontario in conjunction with Chart.js to make an easilly accessible graph to show popularity of names between the years of 1917 and 2010. (This is the data available)

What can I use this for?

There are a lot of interesting trends that appear when looking at names over time. One particularly interesting thing I like to look at is names compared to movies. For example, take a look at the results for the name "Carrie", and the check out this IMDB page.
